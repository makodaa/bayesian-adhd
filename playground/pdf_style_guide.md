# PDF Report Style Guide

Visual and typographic description of the BayesADHD EEG Assessment Support Report as rendered by `backend/app/services/pdf_service.py`.

---

## Page Layout

- **Paper size:** A4
- **Margins:** 20 mm left/right/top, 25 mm bottom (reserved for footer)
- **Content width:** 170 mm

---

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Primary | `#5a6c7d` | Signatory section header text |
| Secondary | `#6c9bd1` | (available; not currently applied to body) |
| Body text | `#333333` | General value text |
| Light gray background | `#f5f7fa` | (available; not currently applied to body) |
| Border | `#e0e0e0` | Horizontal rules, table grids |
| Warning / amber | `#f0ad4e` | Moderate confidence bar fill |
| Success / green | `#5cb85c` | High confidence bar fill |
| Danger / red | `#d9534f` | Low confidence bar fill |

---

## Typography

All body text uses Helvetica (PDF built-in). No external fonts are loaded.

| Style name | Font | Size | Weight | Color | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `ReportTitle` | Helvetica | 20 pt | Bold (Heading1 parent) | Black | Left-aligned; report title only |
| `SectionHeader` | Helvetica | 12 pt | Bold (Heading2 parent) | Black | 8 pt space before, 4 pt space after |
| `Subsection` | Helvetica | 11 pt | Bold (Heading3 parent) | Black | 6 pt space before, 3 pt space after |
| `ReportBody` | Helvetica | 10 pt | Regular | Black | 12 pt leading; 2 pt space before/after |
| `Disclaimer` | Helvetica | 9 pt | Regular | Black | 11 pt leading; used in Intended Use box |
| `Classification` | Helvetica | 12 pt | Regular | Black | Left-aligned; legacy style, superseded |
| `Footer` | Helvetica | 8 pt | Regular | Gray | Center-aligned; legacy, superseded by FooterCanvas |
| `_ClassLabel` (inline) | Helvetica | 14 pt | Bold | Black | Classification result label |
| `_DisclaimerHeading` (inline) | Helvetica | 14 pt | Bold | Black | Disclaimer box heading |
| `_DQFlagHeading` (inline) | Helvetica | 11 pt | Bold | Black | Data quality flags box heading |
| `_LimitationItem` (inline) | Helvetica | 10 pt | Regular | Black | Numbered limitation items |
| Footer canvas (left) | Helvetica | 7 pt | Regular | Gray | Disclaimer text, bottom-left |
| Footer canvas (right) | Helvetica | 8 pt | Regular | Gray | Page N of Total + version, bottom-right |

---

## Page Footer

Every page carries a two-part footer drawn by `FooterCanvas` at y = 10 mm:

- **Left (18 mm from left edge, 7 pt gray):** Static disclaimer — "This report is generated by an automated support tool and does not constitute a clinical diagnosis. All findings require interpretation by a qualified healthcare professional."
- **Right (192 mm from left edge, 8 pt gray, right-aligned):** `Page N of Total | BayesADHD vX.X`

The footer is rendered via `doc.multiBuild()` (two-pass) so the total page count is accurate on all pages.

## Section Order and Visual Treatment

### 1. Header

- Report title (`ReportTitle`, 20 pt bold black)
- Two-column info table (40 mm label + 130 mm value): Report Generated, Assessment Date, Reference, System, System Version
  - Labels: `Helvetica-Bold`, 9 pt, `#666666`
  - Values: `Helvetica`, 9 pt, `#666666`
- Horizontal rule (`#e0e0e0`, 1 pt)

### 2. Intended Use

- `SectionHeader`: "Intended Use"
- Single-paragraph box:
  - Background: `#fafafa`
  - Border: 0.5 pt solid `#999999`
  - Padding: 10 pt all sides
  - Text: `Disclaimer` style (9 pt)

### 3. Disclaimer

- No section header — the box itself carries the heading
- Outer box:
  - Background: `#fff8e1` (pale amber)
  - Border: 1 pt solid `#e6a817` (amber)
  - Padding: 10 pt all sides
- Heading inside box: "⚠ AUTOMATED ANALYSIS — NOT A CLINICAL DIAGNOSIS" — 14 pt Helvetica-Bold, black
- Body inside box: `ReportBody` style (10 pt)

### 4. Subject Information

- `SectionHeader`: "Subject Information"
- Two-column table (50 mm label + 120 mm value): Subject Code, Age, Gender
  - Labels: `Helvetica-Bold`, 10 pt, black
  - Values: `Helvetica`, 10 pt, black
  - Cell padding: 6 pt top/bottom

### 5. Recording Conditions & Technical Parameters

- `SectionHeader`: "Recording Conditions & Technical Parameters"
- **Environmental Context** (`Subsection`, 11 pt bold): File Name, Sleep Hours, Food Intake, Caffeinated, Medicated, Medication Details (conditional), Notes (conditional)
- **Technical Parameters** (`Subsection`, 11 pt bold): Montage, Confirmed Channels, Preprocessing Applied
- Both sub-tables: 50 mm label + 120 mm value; `Helvetica-Bold` labels, 10 pt, top-aligned

### 6. Data Quality Flags (conditional)

Rendered only when one or more of six trigger conditions are met (sleep ≤ 5 h, caffeine, medication, confidence < 60%, missing demographics, channels < 19).

- Outer box: background `#fff8e1`, border 1 pt `#e6a817` (same amber as disclaimer)
- Heading: "⚠ Data Quality Considerations" — 11 pt Helvetica-Bold, black
- Bullets: `ReportBody` style bullet list

### 7. Classification Result

- `SectionHeader`: "Classification Result"
- Classification label: 14 pt Helvetica-Bold, black
- Confidence line: `ReportBody` (10 pt) — `Confidence: XX.X% — <tier>`
- Confidence bar (ReportLab `Drawing`):
  - Track: 120 mm wide × 8 mm tall, fill `#f0f0f0`, stroke 0.5 pt `#cccccc`
  - Fill rect: width proportional to confidence %; color = green / amber / red per tier
  - Percentage label: 8 pt Helvetica, black, to the right of the track

**Confidence tier colors:**

| Tier | Threshold | Bar fill |
|---|---|---|
| High Confidence | ≥ 80% | `#5cb85c` (green) |
| Moderate Confidence | 60–79% | `#f0ad4e` (amber) |
| Low Confidence | < 60% | `#d9534f` (red) |

### 8. Clinical Interpretation

- `SectionHeader`: "Clinical Interpretation"
- Gray box:
  - Background: `#f0f4f8`
  - Border: 0.5 pt `#c0cdd8`
  - Padding: 10 pt all sides
- Three paragraphs (`ReportBody`, 10 pt): classification meaning, confidence context, static reminder; 4 pt spacer between each

### 9. Arousal & Attention Profile

- `SectionHeader`: "Arousal & Attention Profile"
- Gray box (same style as Clinical Interpretation: `#f0f4f8` / `#c0cdd8`, 10 pt padding)
- Single narrative paragraph: `ReportBody` (10 pt)

### 10. Relative Band Power Analysis

- `SectionHeader`: "Relative Band Power Analysis"
- Chart title (`Subsection`): "Averaged Relative Band Power Across All Channels"
- **Horizontal bar chart** (ReportLab `HorizontalBarChart`):
  - Canvas width: 170 mm; x-origin: 50 mm; chart width: 110 mm
  - Bar shades (print-safe grayscale):

| Band | Shade hex | Symbol |
|---|---|---|
| Delta | `#111111` (near-black) | ■ |
| Theta | `#444444` (dark gray) | ◼ |
| Alpha | `#777777` (mid gray) | ▪ |
| Beta | `#aaaaaa` (light gray) | □ |
| Gamma | `#cccccc` (pale gray) | ░ |

  - Bar stroke: 0.5 pt black
  - Category axis: 9 pt Helvetica, right-anchored labels
  - Value axis: 8 pt Helvetica, `%.0f%%` format, light gray grid lines (`#e0e0e0`)
  - Value labels: 8 pt Helvetica, black, appended to right of each bar

- **Page break** inserted after chart
- **Summary table** (4 columns: symbol indicator | Band | Frequency Range | Relative Power):
  - Header row: `Helvetica-Bold`, background `#f0f0f0`
  - Body: 9 pt Helvetica, black
  - Full grid: 0.5 pt black
  - Column widths: 8 mm | 42 mm | 60 mm | 60 mm
  - Relative Power column: right-aligned

### 11. Clinical Ratios

- `SectionHeader`: "Clinical Ratios"
- Three-column table (65 mm | 25 mm | 80 mm): Ratio Name | Value | Clinical Note
  - Header row: `Helvetica-Bold`, background `#f0f0f0`
  - Body: 9 pt Helvetica, black; Value column right-aligned; all cells top-aligned
  - Full grid: 0.5 pt black; padding 6 pt top/bottom, 8 pt left/right
- **TBR footnote** (conditional — shown when `theta_beta_ratio` is present): italic `ReportBody` paragraph citing Monastra et al., 2001; Arns et al., 2013

### 12. General Clinical Considerations

- `SectionHeader`: "General Clinical Considerations"
- Italic preamble paragraph (`ReportBody`)
- Bullet list (`ReportBody`): class-specific bullet items

### 13. EEG Signal Visualizations (conditional)

- **Page break** before section
- `SectionHeader`: "EEG Signal Visualizations"
- Introductory paragraph: `ReportBody`
- Per-band subsection heading (`Subsection`) + embedded image
  - Images scaled to fit within 170 mm × 110 mm, preserving aspect ratio
  - 8 pt spacer below each image
- Displayed bands in order: Raw, Filtered, Delta, Theta, Alpha, Beta, Gamma, Segments

### 14. System Limitations and Scope of Use

- `SectionHeader`: "System Limitations and Scope of Use"
- 8 numbered items; each rendered as a single paragraph:
  - **Bold:** `N. Title`
  - Regular: body text
  - `_LimitationItem` style (10 pt, 4 pt space before, 2 pt after)

### 15. Signatory Block

- Horizontal rule (`#e0e0e0`, 1 pt), preceded by 20 pt spacer
- Two-column table (85 mm | 85 mm):
  - Header row: `Helvetica-Bold`, color `#5a6c7d`
  - Left column: Prepared By (clinician name + occupation), date
  - Right column: blank-line fields for reviewer Name, Designation, Signature, Date
  - Body text: `#333333`, 10 pt; padding 8 pt top/bottom
- Italic acknowledgment note (`ReportBody`) below table

## Consistent Table Conventions

- Standard data tables (Subject, Recording): 50 mm label + 120 mm value; `Helvetica-Bold` labels
- Header info table: 40 mm label + 130 mm value; 9 pt `#666666`
- Grid tables (Band Powers summary, Ratios): full 0.5 pt black grid; `#f0f0f0` header row
- Box tables (Intended Use, Disclaimer, Clinical Interpretation, Narrative, Data Quality Flags): single outer `Table` with background + border styling; inner content `Table` for padding isolation
