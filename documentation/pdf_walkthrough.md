# PDF Feature Walkthrough

This guide explains what each part of the PDF report is meant to communicate, so readers know what to expect at a glance.

## What the PDF Provides

The report is a clinical summary of a single EEG assessment for ADHD. It highlights the subject details, recording context, band power profile, key findings, model interpretation, and required clinical disclaimer, then closes with signatories and a footer for tracking.

## Report Sections and Purpose

### Header
- **What you see:** The report title, institution, and the report date.
- **What it tells you:** Which organization produced the report and when it was generated.
- **Sample:** "Electroencephalogram (EEG) Report" | "BayesianADHD" | "Date: 03/16/2026"

### Subject Information
- **What you see:** Subject code, age at assessment, and gender.
- **What it tells you:** Who the report is for, without identifying personal details.
- **Sample:** "Subject Code: C03-08F-003" | "Age at Assessment: 10 years" | "Gender: Female"

### Assessment Information
- **What you see:** Assessment ID, recording ID, and technician placeholder in a gray bar, followed by medication, alertness, and sensor group.
- **What it tells you:** The recording context and any clinical conditions that may affect interpretation.
- **Sample:** "Assessment ID: 1 | Recording ID: 1 | Technician: Not recorded" and "Medication at referral: None"

### Relative Band Powers
- **What you see:** A two-column block with band labels and percent values on the left, and a horizontal bar profile on the right.
- **What it tells you:** The overall spectral shape and exact band percentages at a glance.
- **Sample:** "Delta (0.5-4 Hz) Properties: 68.95%" with a long bar; "Gamma (30-100 Hz) Properties: 0.91%" with a short bar.

### Spectral Findings
- **What you see:** Direct statements (e.g., “Reduced alpha power (5.11%).”) with brief interpretation below.
- **What it tells you:** How each band compares to expected patterns and what that may imply.
- **Sample header:** "Reduced alpha power (5.11%)."
- **Sample interpretation:** "Below normative alpha range of 8.0-12.0%. Reduced posterior alpha; may indicate heightened cortical arousal or hyperactive/impulsive state."

### Model Classification
- **What you see:** A single sentence summarizing the model’s interpretation and confidence.
- **What it tells you:** The automated classification outcome in plain language.
- **Sample:** "The spatiotemporal model found the recording to have features supportive of ADHD - Inattentive Type. High confidence (99.9%)."

### Summary of Findings / Diagnostic Significance / Clinical Comments
- **What you see:** Three short boxed sections in the left column.
- **What it tells you:** A concise wrap-up, clinical meaning, and any notes.
- **Sample Summary:** "Relative band power summary: Delta 68.95%, Theta 14.02%, Alpha 5.11%, Beta 4.85%, Gamma 0.91%."
- **Sample Diagnostic Significance:** "Model classification: ADHD - Inattentive Type (99.9% confidence). Clinical correlation is required."
- **Sample Clinical Comments:** "Patient cooperative throughout the recording."

### Important Disclaimer
- **What you see:** A boxed disclaimer in the right column.
- **What it tells you:** This report is supportive only and not a definitive diagnosis.
- **Sample:** "This report is generated by a computational EEG analysis tool and is intended to support - not replace - clinical evaluation."

### Signatories
- **What you see:** Signature lines for Technician, Clinician, and Supervising Clinician.
- **What it tells you:** Who reviewed and signed the report.
- **Sample:** "__________________" above "Technician" | "Admin Clinician" above "Administrator" | "__________________" above "Supervising clinician"

### Footer
- **What you see:** Subject Code, Recording ID, Date, and Page number distributed across the width.
- **What it tells you:** How to reference and track the document.
- **Sample:** "Subject Code: C03-08F-003" | "Recording ID: 1" | "03/16/2026" | "Page 1 / 1"

## Notes

- Normative ranges are placeholders (p25/p75) and should be replaced with validated values.
- The PDF layout should always be visually verified before release.
